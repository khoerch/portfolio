Hi there! My name is Kevin and I am a fullstack developer solving problems in Vermont.

ABOUT ME

I am a web developer specializing in React, Node, JavaScript, and complex data sets. Board games inspired my interest in programming. My favorite part of any game is reading and explaining the rules. I approach any web development problem like a complex game -- breaking down each requirement and writing rules to govern that logic.

After four years in Washington, D.C., my partner and I moved to Vermont to be closer to the outdoors. When I’m not working, I’m often snowboarding, climbing, reading comics, or playing Ultimate Frisbee. We make a goal of finding new places to explore each season. I take the same approach to web development. I am always eager to learn something new and make time to explore beyond my comfort zone.

PROJECTS

Title: Spider-Man Quiz App

Project description: This is an early project to combine my experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The goal was to create a simple web app on everyone’s friendly neighborhood Spider-Man. I focused on writing clean, descriptive JavaScript that over users could easily read and understand. The HTML is designed to be as accessible as possible, following a11y best practices. I also hope it inspires some quiz-takers to pick up a comic!
